    About The Kat

    Kit Kat is a chocolate covered wafer bar candy which was originally designed in York, United Kingdom. It was originally created by Rowntree's which was later purchased by Nestle in 1988 which is the company who makes the bar today.The name originates from the 18th century when mutton pies known as Kit Cat were served at meetings of the Kit-Cat Club in London. During the first world war the chocolate was changed to dark chocolate and the packaging was changed to blue due to supply issues. After the war the original milk chocolate returned as well as the now well known red packaging. When Rowntree's was purchased by Nestle in June 1988 they had global control over the distribution of the brand, except in the US where the Hershey company had bought the rights to the chocolate before the company was bought by Nestle. This ownership of distribution is the same to this day.

    Nestlé

    Swiss multinational food and drink company headquartered in Vevey, Switzerland. Originally founded in the 1860's by Henri Nestlé who was originally a local distributor of condensed milk. Since then the Nestlé company has grown internationally and is well known around the globe today
